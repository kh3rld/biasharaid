Frequently Asked Questions

What is BiasharaID?

BiasharaID is a blockchain-based identity verification platform that provides secure and efficient digital identity solutions for businesses and individuals.

How does the identity verification process work?

Our identity verification process involves uploading your identification documents and capturing biometric data. This information is then securely stored and verified using blockchain technology.

Is my data secure with BiasharaID?

Yes, we use advanced blockchain technology and encryption methods to ensure the highest level of security for your personal and business data.
